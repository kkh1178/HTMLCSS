About Thriftygirl

Our founder loves fashion and a good bargain. One of her favorite things to do is to go shopping at bargain-bin, overstock, and thrift stores with her friends, followed by dinner at their favorite Greek restaurant. As a scientist, she also has a passion for saving environment and creating a sustainable way to shop. Fashion is notoriously bad for the environment. The fashion industry is the second largest pollutor of the environment, second only to the oil industry. In some countries, 90% of industrial wastewater is dumped directly into rivers without treatment. Thriftygirl wants to extend the life of our favorite garments and lessen the environmental impact of our fashion choices.
